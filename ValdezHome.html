    Who we are

    We are an ever-growing family of Stanley Valdez members dedicated to providing Stanley Valdez-backed medical help.

    What we do

    You can schedule appointments, have a one- on-one talk with a doctor, or refill your prescriptions all within our application.


    How you can join the Stanley Valdez Family:

    Step 1: Talk to your Doctor

    Ask your doctor if the Stanley Valdez portal is right for you. If your doctor thinks you are a good fit, an email will be sent to you asking you to make an account.

    Step 2: Confirm Email

    From the email link provided by your doctor, sign up to the Valdez Family Medicine portal by creating a username and password.

    Step 3: Log in

    Log into the Stanley Valdez portal to ensure that you have a verifiable connection to the system.

    Step 4: Connect your Provider

    Connect your primary care provider by sending an email to your provider letting them know you have confirmed the sign-up. When they get back to you, all relevant information will be available to you.
